Interests

Since moving to Florida in 2013, I have become fascinated with all things weather related. Hurricanes, the wet and dry season, and just general unpredictability of South Florida weather led me to start pursuing more learning on the subject. I've since become a certified Storm Spotter, built my own weather station that reports barometric pressure, temperature and humidity, and I have a rain gauge outside my apartment that i check every day and record on my website, here.

When I'm not working, or fiddling with my weather station, I can usually be found playing tennis or golf, watching Netflix, hanging out with my friends or cooking. I've recently become very focused on eating healthy and i've put a lot of energy into expanding my cooking capabilities by incorporating slow cookers, pressure cookers, and the grill into my overall repertoire.
